There are two versions of Red Hat JBoss Developer Studio available:

1) Red Hat JBoss Developer Studio bundled as an installer for your 
 platform*

2) Red Hat JBoss Developer Studio bundled with EAP 5.1 as an installer
 for your platform*.

You can download JBDS (1) for free w/o a username and password 
 - simply click the links on the download page [0]; credentials 
 are only required for the version bundled with EAP 5.1 (2). 

[0] https://devstudio.jboss.com/earlyaccess/index.html

To download the EAP-bundled version, you must be an existing EAP 
 or Red Hat JBoss Developer Studio customer. Check the Customer Support 
 Portal (CSP) [1] for more information, or you can sign up for 
 the Early Access program here [2]:

[1] https://access.redhat.com/home

[2] http://bit.ly/jbdevstudioea

* - Windows 32-bit, Linux 32- or 64-bit, Mac OSX 32-bit
